# U.S.-Industrial-Robotics-Market
The U.S. Industrial Robotics Market size was valued at USD 4371 million in 2024 and is expected to reach USD 5219.0 million by 2025. Looking ahead, the industry is projected to expand significantly, reaching USD 9936.7 million by 2030, registering a CAGR of 13.7% from 2025 to 2030.
